Apply the Vermote 1997 adjacency correction in-place to one band.

Computes the environmental reflectance box filter and the direct transmittance, then applies the adjacency correction to every pixel.

Parameters
[in,out]r_boaBOA reflectance float[nrows × ncols], updated in place.
[in]nrowsImage height in pixels.
[in]ncolsImage width in pixels.
[in]psf_radius_kmEnvironmental PSF radius [km] (must be > 0).
[in]pixel_size_mPixel size [m] (must be > 0).

Compute the environmental (spatially-averaged) reflectance map.

Applies a separable box filter of half-width filter_half pixels to the BOA reflectance map. NaN pixels are excluded from the average.

Parameters
[in]r_boaInput BOA reflectance float[nrows × ncols].
[out]r_envOutput environmental reflectance float[nrows × ncols] (must not alias r_boa).
[in]nrowsImage height in pixels.
[in]ncolsImage width in pixels.
[in]filter_halfBox-filter half-width in pixels.
