Detailed Description

Image-based atmospheric state retrievals for i.hyper.atcorr.

All functions are pure computation with no GRASS dependencies. The caller is responsible for allocating output buffers and supplying pre-loaded per-pixel band arrays.

Implemented algorithms:

  • H₂O from 940 nm band depth (Kaufman & Gao 1992)
  • AOD from MODIS dark-target DDV (Kaufman et al. 1997)
  • O₃ from Chappuis band depth at 600 nm
  • Surface pressure from ISA barometric formula or O₂-A band depth
  • Cloud / shadow / water / snow quality bitmask
  • MAIAC-inspired patch-median AOD spatial regularisation
Note
All radiance inputs are in W m⁻² sr⁻¹ µm⁻¹ (= mW m⁻² sr⁻¹ nm⁻¹).

References:

  • Kaufman, Y.J. & Gao, B.C. (1992), IEEE TGRS 30:871–884 (H₂O)
  • Kaufman, Y.J. et al. (1997), IEEE TGRS 35:1286–1298 (AOD DDV)
  • Bogumil, K. et al. (2003), J. Photochem. Photobiol. A 157:167–184 (O₃)
  • ICAO (1993), International Standard Atmosphere (ISA pressure)
  • Schläpfer, D. et al. (1998), SPIE 3502 (O₂-A pressure)
  • Lyapustin, A. et al. (2011), JGR 116:D05203 (MAIAC)

Scene-mean and per-pixel AOD from the MODIS dark-target DDV algorithm.

DDV mask: 0.01 < ρ_2130 < 0.25 AND NDVI(860, 660) > 0.1. Surface reflectance: ρ_surf(470) = 0.25 ρ_2130, ρ_surf(660) = 0.50 ρ_2130. Path reflectance: ρ_path = max(0, ρ_TOA − ρ_surf). AOD inversion: τ = ρ_path × 4μs / (ω₀ P_HG), g=0.65, ω₀=0.89. Ångström interpolation to 550 nm: τ_550 = τ_470 × (550/470)^(−α). Non-DDV pixels are filled with the scene-mean AOD.

Patch-median AOD spatial regularisation (MAIAC-inspired).

Divides the image into non-overlapping patch_sz × patch_sz blocks. Each block's AOD is replaced by its per-block median (robust to DDV outliers). Blocks with no valid AOD are filled by inverse-distance weighting from neighbouring block centroids.

Parameters
[in,out]aod_datafloat[nrows × ncols], updated in place.
[in]nrowsImage height in pixels.
[in]ncolsImage width in pixels.
[in]patch_szBlock side in pixels (typical: 32 for 30 m, 16 for 5 m).
See also
Lyapustin, A. et al. (2011), MAIAC. JGR 116, D05203.

Per-pixel cloud / shadow / water / snow quality bitmask.

Classifies each pixel using TOA reflectance thresholds (converted from radiance using sixs_E0() and the Earth–Sun distance at doy):

  • Cloud: TOA_blue > 0.25 AND NDVI < 0.2, OR TOA_nir > 0.60
  • Shadow: TOA_blue < 0.04 AND TOA_red < 0.04 AND TOA_nir < 0.04
  • Water: TOA_nir < 0.05 AND NDVI < 0.0
  • Snow: NDSI > 0.4 AND TOA_nir > 0.1 (only when L_swir ≠ NULL)
Parameters

[in]L_redPer-pixel TOA radiance near 660 nm [npix].

[in]L_swirPer-pixel TOA radiance near 1600 nm [npix]; NULL → skip snow.
[in]npixNumber of pixels.
[in]doyDay of year [1, 365] (for Earth–Sun distance).
[in]sza_degSolar zenith angle [degrees].
[out]out_maskPre-allocated uint8_t[npix]; bitmask per pixel.
